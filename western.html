Dancing in Western

The word Ballet comes from the Italian word Ballare (dance) and from the old Latin word Ballo.
Ballet was originally a kind of European mass self-entertainment or square performance of the
dance, in the process of development has formed a strict norms and deconstruction of the form,
its main feature is that the actress to wear special toe shoes stand toe dance. As a comprehensive
stage art, ballet originated in Italy, the 17th century in France court formation. French court ballet
is under a unified theme, singing, dancing, music, recitation and drama plot into one.
