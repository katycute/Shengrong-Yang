Dance

Dance is a kind of performing arts, using the body to complete a variety of elegant or difficult movements,
generally with music accompaniment, rhythmic movements as the main means of expression of the art form. It usually
uses music and other props. Dance itself has multiple social significance and functions, including sports, social
contact/courtship, sacrifice, etiquette and so on. Before the dawn of civilization, dance was important in ceremonies,
rituals, ceremonies and entertainment. Dance appeared in China five thousand years ago. It originated from the slave society
and formed its own characteristics by the time of the qin and han dynasties.
